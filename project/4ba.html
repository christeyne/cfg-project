FOUR

You scream, “STOOOPP!” at your attacker in your most piercing tone possible.

He promptly drops you at the sound of your voice. You turn around quickly to face him and see him putting his weapon away.

“Please don’t scream,” he says, wincing. “You don’t want to ruin that voice. You’ve got lots of fans, you know, who are still waiting to hear you sing.”

“If you don’t let me go,” you say, feeling rather courageous, “I will cut my vocal chords, post a photo on Mirror and blame you for it. See if my fans don’t hunt you down after that, Huntsman.”

He sighs, resigned. “Fine. But word of warning, you might want to be careful with that phone.”


CHOOSE

A. You realise that having your phone on you could be risky. B. You disregard his advice because why would he help you? He’s probably trying to keep you lost and wandering in the forest forever.
