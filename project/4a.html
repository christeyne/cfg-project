You struggle immediately, panicking.

Instead of slicing your throat out like you expected, however, your attacker drops the knife but keeps a firm hold of your shoulder and swivels you around.

“Snow White?” He asks.

You don’t respond, but he looks like he already knows who you are.

He sighs. “Look, princess, I’m sure word’s already got around, but I’m the Huntsman. The Queen hired me to kill you.”

You stare up at him defiantly. Tell you something you don't know, why doesn’t he?

As if in response to your thoughts, he looks down at you and sighs again, looking rather reluctant. “Right, here’s the thing. My daughter and I saw that video of you singing that you uploaded to Mirror the other day. We both loved it and I think it would be a huge shame if all that talent was wasted because of the Queen.”

Oh. That was unexpected.

He lets go of you to grab a photo of you from his pocket and a marker. “I’ll let you free on two conditions: remember me when you make it big; and sign this for my daughter and I.”

You’re ridiculously grateful for his offer (good thing you uploaded that singing video!) and you’d be stupid not to take it, so you sign the photo and turn to leave.

“Oh,” he adds and you pause to hear him out. “You might want to be careful with that phone of yours."

Choose:

A. You give your phone to the Huntsman to let him get rid of it.

B. You keep your phone on you. You’ll be undetectable so long as you keep your 3G off, right?
