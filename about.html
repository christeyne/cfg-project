About This Project



This choose-your-own-adventure story follows the story of @snowwhite. Drawing on the classic fairytale of snowwhite and the seven dwarfs, in this story you are also deeply embedded in a digital culture of social media. With every choice you make, you are navigating a dangerous and magical world, helping @snowwhite to find her one true love.

This website was developed as part of the Code First Girls Introduction to Web Development Course by Christine Hui and Viktoria Beran.
